Readme & Usage Guide

This tool helps you estimate capacity and costs for Vertex AI Generative AI models across different traffic scenarios (Average, Peak, Low). It allows you to compare PayGo and Provisioned Throughput (PT) options.

Estimation Modes:

  • Normal workload definition - Define full workload per query and QPS for different usage tiers.
  • Estimate using only tokens per second - Locks QPS to 1. Inputs are treated directly as units per second. Good when you already know your throughput requirements.
  • Quick ballpark - Similar to normal workload definition but simplifies results to 4 key comparisons.

Custom Scenarios:

Custom scenarios allow you to explore hybrid cost models by setting a fixed Provisioned Throughput baseline (in GSUs) and using PayGo to cover traffic spikes (spillage). This helps find the most cost-effective balance between commitment and flexibility.

Limitations:

  • Calculations are estimates and assume steady state traffic within the hours defined.
  • Spillage calculations are based on simplified models and may vary in production.

Important Notes

  • All Gemini 3 series models listed are currently in Preview. Pricing and availability are subject to change.
  • "N/A" for Priority PayGo on Image models suggests it's not currently offered for those specific model types.
  • Flex PayGo is only supported on the global endpoint.
  • Token counts for images and video/audio are calculated differently than text. Refer to the official Vertex AI pricing page for details.
  • Overage from Provisioned Throughput is typically handled by the Standard PayGo pool and billed at standard rates.
